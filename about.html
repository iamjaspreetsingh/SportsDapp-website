App Features

True Power to users

Users can post their own questions and can decide the participation fee as well for the voting period.

Test your game knowledge

The app lets you engross more in to the game and test your knowledge too as your win depends upon how well you understand the sport.

Transparent and Verifiable earnings

Blockchain powered application ensures that you get fair chance to win and everyone playing and participating is verifiable by the time stamps of the transactions.

Rewards with ~0% middlemen fees

No third party can earn irrelevant share of the prize money won by the winners. The share of all the funds coming to the system is distributed without taking any large middlemen fees as are seen in centralised apps.
